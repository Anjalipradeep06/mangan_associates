About Us

Mangan & Associates is an emerging challenger brand to traditional recruitment services in Financial Services. We specialise in fuelling momentum for fast-growth Investment Managers, Fund Managers, Superannuation Funds, Insurers, Banks, and FinTechs with exceptional talent.
